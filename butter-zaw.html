🧈 Butter Aroma Zaw

Slow-roasted rice with pure butter — a taste of heritage

Experience the rich taste of tradition with Butter Aroma Zaw, a unique Bhutanese delicacy made from carefully selected rice, roasted slowly with pure butter to bring out its natural aroma and golden flavor.

✦ Perfectly Roasted ✦
Every grain is perfectly roasted to achieve a crispy texture and a deep, buttery fragrance that makes it both satisfying and memorable. Simple, natural, and authentic, Butter Aroma Zaw is a snack that connects you directly to the heart of Bhutanese village life.

Perfect for tea time, travel, or sharing with family and guests, it offers both nourishment and comfort in every bite. Its rich aroma and traditional preparation make it stand out from ordinary snacks, giving you a truly local and authentic experience.

Produced by hardworking villagers of Tshokona, this product carries the story of dedication, tradition, and honest craftsmanship. By choosing Butter Aroma Zaw, you are not only enjoying a premium traditional snack but also supporting local farmers and helping sustain rural livelihoods.

"Taste the difference of real tradition. Simple ingredients. Authentic flavor. Pure Bhutanese heritage."

Butter Aroma Zaw is more than a snack — it is tradition you can taste.
