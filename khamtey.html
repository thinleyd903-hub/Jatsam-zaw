🌾 Khamtey Rice

Pure heritage from the mountains of Bhutan

Grown in the peaceful valleys and mountain fields of Bhutan, Khamtey rice is more than just food. It is a tradition carefully preserved through generations of hardworking farmers who have long depended on the land for their livelihood.

Cultivated with patience, clean mountain water, and the natural rhythm of village life, Khamtey rice carries a taste that reflects the purity and simplicity of Bhutanese culture. Every grain represents dedication, resilience, and the deep connection between the people and the land they call home.

✦ Naturally Grown ✦
Unlike mass produced rice, Khamtey rice is grown with care by local farmers using traditional methods passed down through generations. Its natural aroma, rich texture, and authentic flavor make it a valued part of everyday meals and cultural traditions across Bhutan.

For many families, rice farming is not only a source of income but also a way of life. Long hours are spent preparing the fields, planting, harvesting, and protecting the crops through changing seasons. Yet despite the hard work behind every harvest, many farmers receive only small returns through middlemen and local markets.

Through this platform, we hope to bring Khamtey rice directly from the hands of the villagers to the people who truly value authentic local products. By supporting Khamtey rice, you are not only choosing quality and tradition, but also supporting the hardworking families who continue to preserve Bhutan's agricultural heritage.

"Every grain tells a story of the mountains, the fields, and the people of Bhutan."

Khamtey rice is not simply a product. It is culture, tradition, hard work, and the spirit of village life carried from our homes to yours.
